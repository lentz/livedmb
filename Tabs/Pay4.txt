Intro-

Dave plays different rhythms during this song, and they are more complex when
he is not singing.  The general rhythm is to play the A, D, and G strings
once, then on the upstroke, play the G and D strings for each chord.  I will
just write out the tab for the chord, not the rhythm.  Listen to any version,
and you'll pick up some cool things to do with it.  The intro is repeated at
the end as well.
