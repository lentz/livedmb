The Jimi Thing is not your typical Dave Matthews Band fan website. It is the mother of all unofficial sites pertaining to the Dave Matthews Band. We offer nearly 100 FULL live songs on our server which you can listen to while you surf, such as Jimi Thing, Ants Marching, Tripping Billies, Drive in Drive Out, Lover Lay Down, Lie in Our Graves, Warehouse, #41, and many more. Not only can you find all of Dave Matthew's Band studio songs, but also live, rare, never before heard versions of all your favorite songs. The essence of the Dave Matthew's band is not in their studio music, but in their incredible live performances. This is why The Jimi Thing has three or four versions of many songs such as So Much to Say, Typical Situation, Drive In Drive Out, Crash, Rapunzel, and many more. You can even get in on the live concerts in our growing trading section if you have any CD shows you want to trade. For those of you who wish to find out "What Did He Say", The Jimi Thing has a large selections of lyrics for you to decipher Dave's frequent mumbling. The Jimi Thing Message Board This is where you can post anything you want to request or tell other DMB fans. Discuss songs, lyrics, trade CD's, Tour Dates, or just talk about the band. Don't forget to sign our guestbook!

//News: 2.2.01\\

- Studio Album News The new studio album has been officially completed and will be released on Tuesday, February 27. The album features 12 completely new songs from the band. The song titles are as follows:
1. I Did It
2. When the World Ends
3. The Space Between
4. Dreams of Our Fathers
5. So Right
6. If I Had It All
7. What You Are
8. Angel
9. Fool to Think
10. Sleep to Dream Her
11. Mother, Father
12. Everyday


- Support DMB's Lillywhite Productions

     With the support of many fans around the world, I've started a campaign with the goal to have the Lillywhite recordings released in some way, shape, or form - however the band sees fit. We all appreciate the band's decision to go a new direction, but at the same time we appreciate the hard-work, talent, and spirit put into the Lillywhite recordings; furthermore many fans love the new road-tested songs and would die to hear studio versions - as well as going out to buy a copy of Everyday, of course.

- Pankaj Arora
