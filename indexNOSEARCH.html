The Jimi Thing is not your typical Dave Matthews Band fan website. It is the mother of all unofficial sites pertaining to the Dave Matthews Band. We have over 70 Real Audio files on our server which you can listen to in their entirety, such as Jimi Thing, Ants Marching, Tripping Billies, Drive in Drive Out and many more. Not only can you find all of Dave Matthew's Band studio songs, but also live, rare, never before heard versions of all your favortite songs. You can even get in on the live concerts in our growing trading section if you have any you want to trade CDs. For those of you who wish to find out "What Did He Say", The Jimi Thing has a large selections of lyrics for you to decipher Dave's lyrics. Up and coming is a Dave Matthews Band site must have, Dave Speak!. This section is where you can submit and share your favorite hilarious Dave Matthews quotes from any live performances you may have attended. Be sure to check back for more on the Dave Speak! section.
